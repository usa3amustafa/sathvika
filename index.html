My name is sathvika. I am a student at National Institute of Fashion Technology, currently pursuing Knitwear design course in Hyderabad. I draw my creative inspiration from ancient architecture, Indigenous flora, and chaotic Indian metropolises. In the long run, I envision myself running a suatinable homwgrown brand of ethnic wear in my country. Even though ethnic wear in India is made of woven fabric, I would like to explore different Knits and experiment with the various aspects of textile design.

During my internship at Alamwar textiles, I developed my love for making decorative fabrics, I began experimenting with different mediujs such as cushion covers and tapestry, ever since, I still feel there is more to explore nd will continue to explore with different mediums to continue my leaning journey.

On a personal note, I also want to promote Telanagana Khadi on a global platform and work towards improving rural khadi weavers living conditions and income.
